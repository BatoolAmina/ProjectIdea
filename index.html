Project Proposal: Manas AI

A Multilingual & Secure Platform for Proactive Mental Health Screening. This interactive document outlines the vision, technology, and impact of Manas AI.

The Mandate for Innovation

India faces a profound mental health crisis, defined by a massive treatment gap, deep-seated social stigma, and a critical shortage of professionals. An estimated 150 million people are in need of intervention, with the economic cost projected to hit $1.03 trillion. The current system is overwhelmed, creating an urgent need for a new, technology-led approach.

The Vast Treatment Gap

An estimated 70-92% of individuals with mental disorders in India receive no care, representing millions suffering in silence.

The Wall of Stigma

60% of people believe mental illness is due to a lack of willpower, creating a powerful barrier to seeking help.

Professional Shortage

India has only 0.75 psychiatrists per 100,000 people, far below the WHO recommendation of 3+.

The Manas AI Solution

Manas AI is not a replacement for therapy. It is a **confidential first step**—a "mental fitness thermometer"—designed to empower individuals with self-awareness in a private, stigma-free environment, bridging the gap to professional care.

The "Manas AI" Difference: A Competitive Edge

Aspect Manas AI Approach The Common Approach
Language Multilingual by Design: Built for the linguistic diversity of India. English-Centric: Most tools ignore vernacular languages.
Analysis Multi-modal Fusion: Combines *what* is said (text) and *how* it is said (voice) for deep, contextual understanding. Single-modal: Analyzes only text, missing crucial emotional cues.
Technology Custom Deep Learning: In-house models for fine-tuning and privacy. External APIs: Relies on generic, black-box services.
Security Privacy as a Feature: End-to-end encryption and user data controls are core to the design. An Afterthought: Security is often weak or ignored.

Core Features & Functionality

Manas AI is built on a foundation of unique features designed to provide a trustworthy, accessible, and insightful experience.

🗣️

Multilingual Chat & Voice

Express yourself in your native language (e.g., Hindi, Tamil) via text or voice. The AI understands both.

🔄

Dual-Input Fusion Engine

Combines insights from text and voice for a more accurate and holistic emotional assessment.

🧠

Custom AI Models

In-house deep learning models, not generic APIs, for greater control, privacy, and performance.

🔐

Secure Authentication

Modern login system using JWT and password hashing to protect user accounts from unauthorized access.

📈

Personalized Dashboard

Track your emotional trends over time with a private timeline chart, helping you identify patterns.

💡

Provides simple explanations for its findings (e.g., "Anxious state detected due to rapid speech").

🛡️

Data Privacy Controls

Features like "Informed Consent" and a "Delete My Data" option give you full control over your information.

Architecture & Technology Stack

Manas AI is built on a modern, scalable, and secure microservices architecture, ensuring reliability and performance.

User Interface

A responsive and intuitive user experience is paramount. The frontend is built to be fast, accessible, and calming, providing a seamless interaction on any device.

Framework: Next.js (React)

Styling: Tailwind CSS

Charts: Chart.js

🖥️

API Gateway & User Services

The backend handles user authentication, session management, and acts as a secure gateway to the AI services, ensuring data integrity and fast response times.

Framework: Node.js (Express)

Database: PostgreSQL

Security: JWT, bcrypt, HTTPS

⚙️

The Brain

This dedicated service houses our custom-trained models. It performs the complex text and voice analysis, separated from the main app for maximum performance.

Framework: Python (FastAPI)

ML Libraries: PyTorch/TensorFlow

Models: IndicBERT, CNNs

18-Month Project Roadmap

A phased plan ensures a robust foundation, iterative improvement through real-world data, and a successful public launch.

Months 1-6

Phase 1: MVP & Foundation

Develop the core app with secure login and a functional UI. Train foundational AI models on public datasets for English and Hindi.

Months 7-12

Phase 2: Closed Beta & Refinement

Launch a private beta to ethically collect anonymized data. Use this data to significantly improve model accuracy and expand language support.

Months 13-18

Phase 3: Public Launch & Scaling

Launch the app publicly on app stores. Monitor performance, gather user feedback, and continuously improve the platform and its AI models.

Research & Academic Contribution

This project is not just an application; it's a research platform. The work can be the basis of a significant academic paper, contributing valuable insights to the global scientific community.

Potential Research Paper Topics

  • A comparative study on the accuracy of multi-modal (text + voice) vs. single-modal analysis for mental health screening in Indic languages.
  • An investigation into the challenges and strategies for developing ethically-sound mental health technologies for non-English speaking populations.
  • A proposed framework for building privacy-preserving AI systems for sensitive health data, compliant with the DPDP Act.

Ethical Framework & Data Privacy

Handling sensitive health data is a profound responsibility. Our commitment to trust and safety is built on a non-negotiable foundation of security and ethics.

⚖️

Legal Compliance

Strict adherence to India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP), 2023, ensuring lawful consent, data minimization, and full user rights.

End-to-end encryption (AES-256) for all data in transit and at rest. Your private information remains private, always.
